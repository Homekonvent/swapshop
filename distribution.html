Swapshop distribution events:

The Swapshop distribution events serve the purpose to redistribute the collected material among a new wave of international students who rent a room from the Housing Department. We organise a distribution event at the start of each semester, around the time the check-ins take place.

We try to provide as much students as possible with a package*, but sadly it’s not (yet) possible to hand out a package to all students. Therefore you may want to be quick to fill in the [doodle] which is linked in the email sent by Accomodation. First come, first served!


*Disclaimer:
It may happen that not every package we distribute has the same content. We try to prepare the packages as equal as possible, but due to the varying inventory, that's not always the case.
