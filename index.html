Goal of the Swapshop project:

The Swapshop project aims to reduce the amount of utensils and material that are newly bought every semester by incoming international students and then are simply thrown away after only a couple months being used. We do this by collecting and redistributing the utensils that are still in good condition. That way, we try to avoid that near-perfect kitchenware and appliances, cleaning equipment, etc., are thrown away. It’s a WIN-WIN for everybody: you can get rid of the items you don’t want to take home, others get some free utensils and material and we all contribute a little bit to help save the environment!
